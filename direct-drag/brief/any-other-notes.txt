GOAL
- every deal row clicks through to the original listing
- show floor-aggregate confidence next to each price
- every control works or is removed; no placeholders

AUDIENCE
- solo operator, desktop-only, UK fix-and-flip reseller, ten-hour days at a dense terminal

LAYOUT
- seven-page sidebar: dashboard, inventory, analytics, brain, mesh, agentconsole, settings
- density-first; no hero empty states, no narration panels
- render finished screens, not a style guide

DO NOT
- no translucency, blur, backdrop-filter, or opacity-based fills
- no gradients beyond a beveled edge (highlight top-left, shadow bottom-right)
- no SF Symbols; use 16px bitmap icons or hand-authored SVG
- caps or lowercase only; never capitalised-word headings
- no gamification vocabulary (no win-rate card, no XP, no level-ups, no badges)
- no developer-voice UI copy; address the user
- no AI-image filler boxes; no generative-image residue
- no target-platform column on deal rows; resale default is eBay
- no default-visible dismissed or negative-profit rows
- no Matter font; no fonts from the style-lab folder

DO
- dark dense foundation; per-scheme accent ramps per page
- retro Mac OS 9 + cyberpunk + brutalist: hand-drawn bevels, thick borders, tabular numerics
- Satoshi for display, JetBrains Mono for data, IBM Plex Sans for body
- corner radius 4px max; spacing from {4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32}
- curve-and-bounce motion (easeOutBack); transitions under 300ms; ambient loops under 2s

CONTENT
- real data shapes: deals, inventory, floor and target prices, source badges
- six UK vendor badges: ebay, cex, gumtree, facebook, shpock, preloved, wholesale-clearance
- CeX buys only (never sell target); eBay is source, sold-comps, and sell venue
- console hardware in; console games out

DELIVERABLES
- two or three variations per page
- prefer Claude-Code handoff bundles
- no narrating-state copy

VOICE
- direct labels; plain English; no preambles
