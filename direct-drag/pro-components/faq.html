can i change plans at any time?

any change takes effect at the start of the next billing cycle. prorated credit is applied automatically for mid-cycle upgrades; downgrades keep the current tier until the cycle ends so you don't lose paid-for days.

do you offer a free trial?

every plan includes a 14-day trial, no card required. at the end of the trial you can pick a paid plan, stay on the free tier, or walk away with no data loss — exports are available for 30 days after cancellation.

is my data encrypted in transit and at rest?

yes. all data moves over tls 1.3 and is stored encrypted with aes-256. enterprise workspaces can add customer-managed keys (cmek) and per-region data residency (eu-west, us-east, ap-southeast).

how do exports work?

everything exports: design tokens as css / tailwind / style dictionary, components as html / react / vue, assets as png / webp / avif, and your whole workspace as a json archive you can import elsewhere.

do you support single sign-on?

saml and oidc are available on the team plan. scim 2.0 user provisioning ships with enterprise. tested against okta, jumpcloud, google workspace, entra id, and auth0.

can i self-host?

self-hosted deployments are available to enterprise customers with a signed msa. the package runs on kubernetes 1.27+ and includes its own postgres, object store, and redis layers. estimated install time is half a day with a dedicated devops engineer.

what happens if i cancel?

your workspace stays accessible in a read-only state for 30 days. during that window you can export, invite a new owner, or re-subscribe without losing history. after 30 days the workspace is permanently deleted.

is there a non-profit discount?

yes — a 50% discount applies to registered 501(c)(3) organisations and the uk / eu equivalents. send a link to your charity registration and we'll verify within one business day.

how fast is the search?

p95 latency under 80ms, measured continuously in production and published on the status page.

is it soc 2 audited?

soc 2 type ii report available under nda. iso 27001 in flight, target completion q3.

eu data residency?

yes — enterprise can pin storage and processing to eu-west only. auditable via a single workspace flag.

open api?

rest and graphql documented with openapi 3.1. sdks in typescript, python, go, and ruby.

team plan minimum?

five seats minimum. additional seats prorate to the day, added or removed from the dashboard.

figma → code roundtrip?

token updates in figma open a pr in code. token updates in code push back into figma styles.

annual discount?

17% off when billed annually. switch at any time from the billing page.

support response time?

under 4 hours on team, under 1 hour on enterprise, with dedicated slack channel on the top tier.

migration help?

white-glove migration from zeroheight, storybook, or a figma-only workflow is included on enterprise.
