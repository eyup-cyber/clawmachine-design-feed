pricing faqs

the five questions people ask most during evaluation.

do you charge for viewer-only users?

no. viewer seats are unlimited on every paid tier. we only charge for seats that edit.

what happens when i add or remove seats?

seats are prorated to the day, up or down, visible on the billing page before you confirm.

is there a non-profit / education rate?

50% off for registered non-profits and accredited educational institutions. apply via the contact form.

what's the annual discount?

17% off the monthly price when billed annually. switch between monthly and annual at any time.
